Water Safety - Hyderabad

The IHS has been contributing to the WSP(Water Safety Plan) by organizing expert group meetings and providing inputs for development of health based targets. The Director, IHS serves on the Steering Committee on Water Safety Plans and Saritha K, Head, WQTS, IHS serves on Task Force for Monitoring implementation of Water Safety Plans constituted by the HMWSSB.

Water Quality Lab

Protecting public health by easily accessible tests of water quality. Services include, basic & complete potability tests, bacteriological analysis, ground water potability & specified single or multiple parameter tests. The lab provides independent water quality monitoring of water in identified slum areas supplied by the Hyderabad Metro Water Supply & Sewerage Board
